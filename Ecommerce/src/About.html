About Koenigsegg

Koenigsegg was founded in 1994 by Christian von Koenigsegg, a visionary entrepreneur with an unyielding passion for cars. At the tender age of 22, Christian set out to create a car that would combine cutting-edge technology, performance, and design, a vehicle that would outperform all contemporary supercars. His audacious vision was to establish Koenigsegg as a symbol of excellence in the automotive world, and he pursued it with relentless determination.

Welcome to Koenigsegg – The Pinnacle of Automotive Engineering

Founded in 1994 by Christian von Koenigsegg, Koenigsegg is a Swedish hypercar manufacturer that has redefined the boundaries of automotive engineering. With its unwavering commitment to pushing the limits of innovation, performance, and design, Koenigsegg stands as an iconic brand known for producing some of the most exclusive and powerful hypercars in the world.

Our Philosophy

At Koenigsegg, we believe in challenging the status quo. Our philosophy centers around pushing the boundaries of what's possible, constantly pursuing excellence, and creating groundbreaking vehicles that captivate automotive enthusiasts worldwide. We are driven by passion, precision, and an unyielding pursuit of perfection.

Engineering Marvels

Every Koenigsegg hypercar is a masterpiece of engineering, blending cutting-edge technology, lightweight materials, and aerodynamic prowess to deliver an unparalleled driving experience. From our revolutionary Freevalve engine technology to advanced carbon fiber construction, each vehicle showcases our relentless commitment to technological innovation.

Performance Redefined

Performance is at the core of every Koenigsegg creation. With record-breaking top speeds and acceleration figures that defy physics, our hypercars are among the fastest and most powerful on the planet. Meticulously crafted to deliver the ultimate driving experience, each Koenigsegg is a testament to our dedication to performance engineering.

Exclusivity and Craftsmanship

Craftsmanship lies at the heart of Koenigsegg's identity. Each vehicle is meticulously handcrafted in our state-of-the-art facility in Ängelholm, Sweden, by a team of skilled artisans and engineers. This level of attention to detail ensures that every Koenigsegg is a bespoke work of art, tailored to meet the individual desires of its owner.

Innovation at the Core

At Koenigsegg, innovation is not just a buzzword; it is the driving force behind our success. Our willingness to embrace the unknown and challenge convention sets us apart from other automotive manufacturers.

Environmental Responsibility

We aim to lead the way in producing high-performance cars with a reduced impact on the environment.
